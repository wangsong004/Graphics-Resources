|---|---|---|---|
| SIGGRAPH | Flagship graphics conference | One of the most important places to track the latest work in graphics and interactive techniques | [Official Site](https://www.siggraph.org/) |
| SIGGRAPH Asia | Major flagship conference in Asia | Essential for tracking graphics research and industry activity in the Asia-Pacific region | [Official Site](https://asia.siggraph.org/) |

## Simple Tracking Strategy

### If you work on geometry processing
Track:
- SGP
- SIGGRAPH
- Eurographics
- TOG
- CGF
- CAD
- CAGD

### If you work on rendering
Track:
- SIGGRAPH
- SIGGRAPH Asia
- EGSR
- HPG
- TOG

### If you work on animation or simulation
Track:
- SCA
- SIGGRAPH
- SIGGRAPH Asia
- TOG

### If you work on XR or visualization
Track:
- IEEE VIS
- EuroVis
- IEEE VR
- TVCG
